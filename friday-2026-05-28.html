4. Useful Expressions from the Video

Here are key idioms, collocations, and expressions extracted from the lyrics that you can use in daily life:

"Give or take"
Meaning: Approximately, more or less.
Everyday Example: "The meeting will last about 45 minutes, give or take."
"Just like that"
Meaning: Suddenly, unexpectedly, or without effort.
Everyday Example: "He stood up, packed his bags, and left the company just like that."
"Before they blink an eye"
Meaning: Extremely quickly, in an instant.
Everyday Example: "Don't leave your coffee there; the cat will knock it over before you blink an eye."
"Get your pink slip"
Meaning: To receive an official notice of job dismissal or termination.
Everyday Example: "During the economic recession, hundreds of corporate workers got their pink slips."
"Lord have mercy"
Meaning: An expression used to show surprise, shock, dismay, or distress.
Everyday Example: "Lord have mercy, look at this massive traffic jam! We are going to be late."
