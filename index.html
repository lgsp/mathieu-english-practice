10 Key Expressions & Practical Idioms

Extracted native formulas for discussing high-impact hobbies and lifestyle modifications

01. To undergo a massive transformation

Subir une transformation radicale.

"Picking up martial arts caused him to undergo a massive transformation, physically and mentally."

02. To sharpen one's intellect

Aiguiser/stimuler ses capacités intellectuelles.

"Learning a secondary language isn't just a party trick; it thoroughly sharpens your intellect."

03. To build something from scratch

Construire quelque chose à partir de rien / de zéro.

"Coding as a hobby empowers you to build software applications completely from scratch."

04. To think outside the box

Penser de manière non conventionnelle / faire preuve de créativité.

"Creative hobbies like painting or writing force your brain to think outside the box."

05. To get out of one's comfort zone

Sortir de sa zone de confort.

"Joining a public speaking club is terrifying, but it's the fastest way to get out of your comfort zone."

06. To be a double-edged sword

Être un couteau à double tranchant.

"Consuming content can be a double-edged sword; it either educates you or drains your focus."

07. To gain a fresh perspective

Acquérir un regard neuf / un nouvel angle de vue.

"Traveling alone allowed her to step away from societal loops and gain a fresh perspective on her career."

08. To pay off in the long run

Porter ses fruits à long terme / s'avérer payant avec le temps.

"Cooking healthy meals might feel time-consuming now, but it definitely pays off in the long run."

09. To keep someone grounded

Garder les pieds sur terre / maintenir un équilibre interne.

"Despite his hectic executive routine, a daily gardening session keeps him grounded."

10. To master a high-income skill

Maîtriser une compétence à forte valeur financière.

"The video highlights that learning how to analyze complex data structures is a true high-income skill."
